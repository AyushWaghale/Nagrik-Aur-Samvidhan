Why is the Indian Constitution Important?

The Indian Constitution is a living document that represents the fundamental principles and values of our nation. It establishes justice, liberty, equality, and fraternity as core ideals and safeguards citizens' rights.

By learning about the Constitution, you become aware of your rights and responsibilities as a citizen. These games offer an engaging way to explore India's constitutional values, rights, and the roles we all play in upholding them.
